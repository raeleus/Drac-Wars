"Let me see your hand, my child" The soothsayer draws her finger across a line on your hand. "Oh no! This is a prophetic sign of ill portent! God is greatly displeased with your sinful and lustful ways."
She cowers under her hood and slips into the shadows of the room. You can still see her in the dimly lit space, but she pretends that you can't. You leave awkwardly.
