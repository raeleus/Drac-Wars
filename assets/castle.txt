A gothic castle looms above you. You cautiously approach the doors. Before you reach out to grab the handle, the doors sway open slowly as if guided by an invisible hand. You call out, but no one responds. As you walk in carefully, it feels as if you are engulfed in a sea of dread.

Suddenly, you are engulfed by blue inferno! A dizzying spectacle of lights surrounds you! Then the room turns black. You open your eyes to find yourself in a strange corridor with doors running parallel as far as the eye can see.

In sheer panic, you open the door closest to you. It opens to a green pasture with rolling hills. What sorcery is this? You open another door: a bog swamp. You need to find a way out of here...
