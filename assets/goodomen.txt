"As I look into my crystal ball, I see... a future! A future of bright and gold! Smiles all around. And at the center of it: YOU! Oh, but the vision is too foggy. Fading... Fading... It's... GONE!"
With her last words, the soothsayer faints. Fearing that you failed to notice, she sighs and faints again, dramatically throwing her body off the chair. You leave awkwardly.
